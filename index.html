Milkha Singh Ji – India’s Flying Legend

Biography

Milkha Singh Ji, an Indian track and field sprinter, overcame tragedy to become a national hero. He represented India in three Olympic Games and won gold at the 1958 Commonwealth Games.

Known as the 'Flying Sikh', his life inspired millions and was immortalized in the film Bhaag Milkha Bhaag. His discipline, humility, and determination shaped India's athletic legacy.
